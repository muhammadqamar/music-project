Musiio Search
Every song in the world has thousands of elements that make it unique which can often be hard to describe in words. Limited by keywords such as “rock” or “cinematic” or “gym music”, users’ inability to describe exactly what they are looking for means that often, they are not getting the best user experience, or the most out of your music collection.
With Musiio Search you have the most cutting edge way to search your catalogue - audio referencing. By pasting an audio link, an mp3, or a music track from your catalogue into your search tool, users now no longer need to find the words to describe the track they are looking for. Instead, they are able to use a 'reference track’ or what we call a seed track and can spend time enjoying accurate audio matches rather than being caught in a loop of inaccurate keywords and poor search results.

Meet Our AI
Our Artificial Intelligence has been trained by listening to production quality commercial music databases with over more than 50 million datapoints.
Our AI can ‘listen’ to and tag over over 200,000 new tracks per day. Once ‘heard’ our technology can search through music at a rate of 1m+ tracks in two seconds.
If a human can hear something in the audio (e.g. an instrument, a genre or a tone), we can teach the AI to ‘hear’ it too and recognise it again with greater than 90% accuracy.
Musical tastes differ from one person to another so “users also listened to…” isn’t always the best way to suggest new artists. Our AI ‘listens’ to the audio rather than using a popularity algorithm, so we can match your users’ audio profile with music we know they'll love, getting the best out of your whole catalogue and creating a personalised experience, not an echo chamber.
When building auto-playlists, our AI can be custom-trained to work from a cold start. For playlisting we also offer a process we call ‘collaborative filtering’ which takes the AI and overlays additional information you already have or are collecting such as skips, completes or saved tracks for example. This process over time allows your AI to become more unique and more accurate for every user.
